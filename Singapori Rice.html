Singaporean Rice

Descirption

Singaporean Rice is a famous rice recipe from Singaporean Cuisine. Among many different rice recipes, you would surely feel the uniqueness of taste in Singaporean Rice. It gives a light spicy taste and some uncommon ingredients which make it truly a delight.


Ingredits

  • Chicken boneless (cube cut) 1/2 kg
  • Ginger (julian cut) 2 tbsp
  • Ketchup 2 tsp
  • Soy sauce 1 tsp
  • Orange food color a pinch
  • Cabbage as preferred
  • Carrot 1
  • Capsicum 1
  • Tomato 1
  • Onion 1
  • For Batter:
  • Egg 1
  • Black pepper 1/2 tsp
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Baking powder 1/2 tsp
  • Plain flour 1 tbsp
  • Corn flour 1 tbsp
  • For Spaghetti:
  • Spaghetti 1 fold
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Oil 1 tsp
  • For Rice:
  • Rice 1/2 kg
  • Salt as to taste (or a pinch)
  • For Sauce:
  • Mayonnaise 1/4 cup
  • Cream 1/2 cup
  • Red pepper (crushed) 1 tsp
  • Salt 1/4 tsp
  • Black pepper 1/4 tsp
  • Ketchup 2-3 tbsp
  • Tandoori food color 1/2 tsp
  • For Garnish:
  • Garlic (chopped) 1 pod
  • Green chillies 5-6

    • How To Make

    1. Chop cabbage, capsicum, carrot and onion
    2. Chop the garlic cloves
    3. Slit the green chillies and remove their seeds
    4. For Batter: Mix an egg, black pepper, salt, plain flour and corn flour
      5. Also add baking powder in it and mix well
    5. Marinate the chicken in the above batter
    6. Take oil in a pan and heat it
    7. Fry the chicken in the pan
    8. Deep fry the green chillies
    9. Then fry the chopped garlic, till they get golden brown
    10. Fry capsicum, carrot, cabbage and onion
    11. Add fried chicken and ginger in a pan
    12. Add 2 tsp tomatoe ketchup and 1 tsp soy sauce
    13. Also add a pinch of orange food color
    14. Cook it for a while
    15. For Spaghetti: Add salt and oil in boiling water
    16. Add spaghetti in the water and boil
    17. For Rice: Soak the rice for half an hour
    18. Take water in a pan, add salt in it
    19. Then add rice in above pan and boil them, then drain off the water
    20. For Sauce: Take mayonnaise in a bowl and add cream in it
    21. Also add red pepper, salt and black pepper
    22. Then add ketchup and tandoori food color
    23. Then take a serving rice tray and make a first layer of rice
    24. Then 2nd layer of noodles and add some mayonnaise sauce on it. And serve hot and enjoy the taste.
